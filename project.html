Mission Statement :
School envisions to join the Nation building process by striving hard to impart quality education in an individualised manner by aiming to achieve the Goals of own permanent building with state of the art infrastructural facilities, holistic development of students through wholesome co-scholastic activities and complete set of regular staff to strengthen the hands of administration by 2020 through concerted efforts of all stakeholders. THE BLISS OF GROWTH! THE GLORY OF ACTION! THE SPLENDOUR OF ACHIEVEMENT!
"Yavajjivathi thavad adhithe"

In true spirit of the much acclaimed 'Gurukula' system of education that ensured total personality development through individualised instruction, ABC is a new addition to the glorious legacy. Set amidst the verdant valleys, lush greenery and misty mountains of Coorg (originally called Kodaimalenadu means 'dense forest on steep hill'), it provides the perfect ambience for imparting quality education. Like other schools, we also strive hard to realise the vision laid down.
